## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial period, no "freemium" tricks.
The audit is free because it's a lead-generation tool for Credex.
We're upfront about that.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: We store the tools and spend numbers you enter and the audit
result. We don't store your company name or email until you
voluntarily provide them after seeing the results.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is sourced from the vendor's official pricing page,
with the date verified. We update it weekly. The source URLs are
in our public PRICING_DATA.md on GitHub.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor,
Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies
that overforecast or pivoted. The savings are real.

**Q: What if my stack is already optimal?**
A: We'll tell you that honestly. "You're spending well" is a
valid audit result. We don't manufacture savings that aren't there.
