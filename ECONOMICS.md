# Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Assumptions (show your reasoning):
- Average AI credit purchase through Credex: $2,000 (discounted
  from ~$3,000 list — typical order size for a 10-40 person team)
- Credex margin on resold credits: ~20-30% (conservative estimate
  based on typical software reseller margins)
- Revenue per conversion: $400-600

- Repeat purchase rate: ~40% annually (teams rebuy when credits run out)
- LTV over 2 years: $400 * (1 + 0.4) = ~$560 per converted lead

Estimate: a converted lead (audit → consultation → purchase) is
worth approximately $400-600 in immediate revenue, $500-600 LTV.

CAC for all organic channels: effectively $0 cash, ~2 hrs/week
of community time.

If Credex added $500/month in paid distribution (newsletter
sponsorships, targeted LinkedIn): estimated 200 additional audits,
40 leads → CAC of $12.50/lead, still well below LTV.

## Conversion funnel

Landing page visit → audit completed:      ~20%
Audit completed → email captured:          ~30%
Email captured → consultation booked:      ~15%
Consultation booked → credit purchase:     ~50%

Full funnel: 1000 visitors → 200 audits → 60 emails
             → 9 consultations → 4-5 purchases

Revenue from 1000 visitors: ~4.5 purchases * $500 = $2,250

## What would have to be true for $1M ARR in 18 months

$1M ARR / $500 avg revenue per purchase / 12 months = ~167 purchases/month

Working backward:
- 167 purchases/month requires ~334 consultations (50% close rate)
- 334 consultations requires ~2,227 email leads (15% book rate)
- 2,227 email leads requires ~7,423 completed audits (30% capture)
- 7,423 audits requires ~37,115 monthly visitors (20% completion)

Is 37k monthly visitors achievable in 18 months?

- Month 1-3: 0→5k/month via HN, Reddit, organic sharing
- Month 4-9: 5k→15k/month via Credex customer base, newsletter
sponsorships, SEO from tool usage (each shared audit URL
  is an indexed page)
- Month 10-18: 15k→37k/month via referral loop (shared audit
  URLs drive new users), 1-2 viral moments per quarter

Verdict: aggressive but plausible if the Credex customer email
list is 1,000+ warm contacts and the HN launch hits front page.
The most critical assumption is the Credex customer base size —
if it's 200 people, the math breaks. If it's 2,000, it works.
